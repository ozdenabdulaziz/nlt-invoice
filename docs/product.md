# Product

## Positioning

NLT Invoice is a modern invoicing SaaS for small businesses in Canada with an English-only interface.

## MVP Scope

Included:

- single user
- single company
- customer management
- invoice management
- estimate management
- public share pages
- print / PDF via browser print flow
- basic dashboard
- auth
- onboarding
- subscription gating

Excluded:

- online payments
- multi-company
- multi-user teams
- advanced tax automation
- accountant portal
- bank integrations

## Plans

### Free

- 1 user
- 1 company
- 5 customers
- 5 invoices per month
- 3 estimates per month
- public links
- print / PDF

### Pro

- 29.99 CAD / month
- 1 user
- 1 company
- unlimited customers
- unlimited invoices
- unlimited estimates
- public links
- print / PDF

### Business

- not self-serve in MVP
- contact us CTA
